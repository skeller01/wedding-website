Once you enter our doors you will instantly feel the luxury and splendor that the Marriott Syracuse Downtown exudes. We are located in the heart of Syracuse, making us an ideal hotel for both business and leisure travelers.

The Jefferson Clinton

Discover a unique blend of elegance, history and personalized service at the Jefferson Clinton Hotel. Perfectly located in downtown Syracuse’s Armory Square, these luxury accommodations provide the ultimate in comfort for both business and leisure travelers.

Step inside this remarkable 1927 hotel and delve into its rich history. Shortly after its construction, the Jefferson Clinton Hotel endured several failed ownerships due to the Great Depression. In 2001, new owners restored the building to its past glory and re-opened the hotel with 8 rooms and 60 suites.
